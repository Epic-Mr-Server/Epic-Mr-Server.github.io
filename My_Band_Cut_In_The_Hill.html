Basic Explanation  

I love playing electric bass. I picked it up over the summer in 8th grade. After our previous band Sodium Chloride disbanded at the end of my senior year in high school, we decided to form a new band with the goal of fully recording and distributing an album.

On Febuary 15th 2024, our band released our debut album "Wasting Gas". It includes 14 tracks each recorded, mixed, as mastered by us.
